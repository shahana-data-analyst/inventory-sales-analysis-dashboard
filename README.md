# 📊 Inventory & Sales Analysis Dashboard

## 🚀 Project Overview
An end-to-end Inventory & Sales Analysis Dashboard built in Microsoft Excel to monitor sales performance, inventory efficiency, demand forecasting, and operational health across multiple product categories and regions. 

The dashboard enables business stakeholders to identify inventory risks, analyze sales trends, improve forecasting accuracy, and support data-driven decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem
Retail businesses often struggle with balancing supply and demand, leading to:
* **Overstocking:** Slow-moving products tying up capital.
* **Stock-out Situations:** Lost revenue due to unavailable inventory.
* **Inefficient Allocation:** Uneven regional performance and poor seasonal planning.

This dashboard provides a centralized analytical solution to monitor inventory health and optimize business operations.

## 🧠 Key Business Insights (Data-Driven)
* **Inventory Imbalance:** Overstock units (32K) are **4x higher** than Stock-Out units (8K). This indicates a significant opportunity to reduce holding costs by optimizing supply for slow-moving items.
* **Seasonal Demand Surge:** Sales peak dramatically during the **Winter season (2.5M units sold)**, closely followed by Autumn, while Spring and Summer show slightly lower operational volumes.
* **Regional Disparity:** The **East, North, and South regions** perform consistently well (each crossing 430M in volume), whereas the **West region** lags significantly behind at ~422M, highlighting an underperforming market that needs targeted marketing.
* **Category Dominance:** Furniture (2.51M) and Clothing/Electronics (2.48M each) drive the bulk of the sales, while Toys (1.01M) is the lowest performing category.

## 🛠️ Excel Skills & Features Used
* **Data Cleaning & Transformation:** Power Query, Handling Null Values, Data Standardization.
* **Functions:** VLOOKUP. 
* **Data Modeling & Summary:** Pivot Tables.
* **Data Visualization:** Pivot Charts, Conditional Formatting, Interactive Slicers, and Timelines.

---

## 🔄 Project Workflow
1. **Data Cleaning:** Extracted raw transaction data, handled duplicates, and standardized date formats.
2. **Data Modeling:** Built relational connections between transactions and product categories using `VLOOKUP`.
3. **Analysis:** Created isolated Pivot Tables to aggregate monthly trends, regional splits, and product performance.
4. **Dashboard Layout:** Designed a clean user interface with dynamic slicers for Category, Region, and Seasonality.
